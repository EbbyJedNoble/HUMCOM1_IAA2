My Top 5 Movies List
(Not in order)


1. The Lion King (1994)


It follows the adventures the adventures of the young lion Simba, the heir of his father, Mufasa. Simba's wicked uncle, Scar, plots to usurp Mufasa's throne by luring father and son into a stampede of wildbeasts. But Simba escapes, and only Mufasa is killed. Simba goes on a journey and there he meets his friends Timon and Pumbaa. Simba returns as an adult to take back his homeland from Scar with the help of his newfound friends.




2. Spirited Away (2001)


An animated film about a tale of the fanciful adventures of a ten-year old girl name Chihiro, who discovers a secret world when she and her family got lost and ventured through a hillside tunnel. After her parents underwent a mysterious transformation, Chihiro meets a mysterious boy named Haku who explains that the park is a resort for supernatural beings who need a break from their time spent in the earthly realm, and that she must work there to free both herself and her parents.




3. Finding Nemo (2003)


An animated film about a tale which follows comedic and eventful journeys of two fishes, Marlin and his young son Nemo, who were separated from each other in the Great Barrier Reef when Nemo is unexpectedly taken away from his home and put into a fish tank at a dentist's office. Marlin meets a friendly fish later on named Dory. He embarks on a dangerous and epic journey to rescue his son. Meanwhile, the young Nemo is also on a journey on his own too to try to return to his home safely and reunite with his father.




4. Catch Me If You Can (2002)


A film which is based on a true story about a man named Frank Abagnale Jr. who is a conman, and have successfully conned million of dollars worth of checks before he was even 19 as a fake pilot, doctor, and legal prosecutor. An FBI agent makes it his mission to catch him and put him behind bars, but Frank continues to avoid capture, and successfully fools the FBI many times.




5. Alice in Wonderland (1951)


On a golden afternoon, young Alice follows a White Rabbit, who disappears down a nearby rabbit hole. She quickly follows him, and she tumbles down into the hole. She then finds herself in a mysterious world of Wonderland.
